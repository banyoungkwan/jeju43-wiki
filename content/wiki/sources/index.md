자료실은 제주4·3 연구를 위한 다양한 자료를 체계적으로 정리한 공간입니다. 공식 조사 보고서, 1차 자료, 증언 기록, 학술 자료 등 네 가지 주요 카테고리로 구성되어 있습니다.

The Sources & References section systematically organizes diverse materials for Jeju 4·3 research. The collection is organized into four main categories: Official Reports, Primary Sources, Testimonies, and Academic Sources.

## 자료 카테고리 | Categories

### 보고서 | Official Reports

[[보고서/index|보고서]] includes official investigation reports, government white papers, and international organization documents on memorialization and transitional justice related to Jeju 4·3.

### 1차 자료 | Primary Sources

[[1차자료/index|1차 자료]] contains declassified U.S. government documents, military intelligence reports, diplomatic correspondence, and military advisory reports from the period 1945–1950.

[[1차자료/index|자세히 보기]]

### 증언 | Testimonies

[[증언/index|증언]] documents oral accounts and testimonies from survivors, bereaved families, and witnesses. Materials range from the first testimony collection in 1989 to recent truth-seeking recordings.

[[증언/index|자세히 보기]]

### 학술 자료 | Academic Sources

[[학술/index|학술 자료]] comprises scholarly works including papers, dissertations, and research monographs by both Korean and international researchers examining various aspects of Jeju 4·3.
